## Areas for Improvement
1. **Accessibility**: While the slides use clear language, further accommodations for diverse learning needs (like text alternatives for visual content) could be beneficial for students with disabilities.

4. **Visual Consistency**: Different slides employ various layouts and styles for code examples, which may lead to slight inconsistency in visual coherence.

5. **Concept Reinforcement**: Incorporating more checkpoints for understanding, such as flashcards or review questions at the end of sections, could help reinforce learning.
